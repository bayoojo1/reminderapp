What can I do on NaatCast?
NaatCast is a social platform for broadcasting audio message. You can use NaatCast to send audio message to yourself or anyone. You compose your message as text or upload a pre-recorded audio file. NaatCast delivers the message to your recepient mobile number in audio form. Which means your recepient recieves a call and once the call is picked, he/she would listen to the message. No need for Internet connectivity to receive the call, since it's a normal telephone call. NaatCast ensure the recepient does not miss the call by repeating the call at intervals until it's picked up.
As a registered user, you can follow other users, and you can be followed. You can broadcast message to your followers, and if you wish, you can receive audio broadcast from those you follow. You can subscribe to any content provider of your choice, and request for audio broadcast from the provider. You can also become an audio content provider, provided you have audio content under our various categories. Currently we have the following categories: Educational, Comedy, Sport, Breaking News, Weather Report, Traffic Report, Market Survey, Entertainment, Hot Gist, Religion, Health and Fitness, Food and Nutrition, Fashion and Beauty, Finance, Security, Auto Care, Pet Care.
How do I use NaatCast?
After registration, you have access to your NaatCast profile.
A home page where you can either broadcast a composed text or an uploaded audio file.
You would need to enter the recipient mobile number(s) or select to broadcast to your followers or subscribers if you are a provider.
Select how recurrent you want the broadcast to be, once, daily, weekly, monthly, etc.
Select whether you want the message to be seen only on your timeline or seen on your followers or subscribers timeline.
Select the date and time you want the broadcast to start.
Post your message and leave the rest to NaatCast to handle.
NOTE: Mobile number should be in the format: countrycode+mobile, without the leading zero, e.g 23480XXXXXXXX
What type of audio file and size can I upload?
We currently allow .wav and .mp3 file format. You can also upload up to 5MB of file size and no more.
How much does it cost to share a broadcast?
A minute broadcast would cost you NGN50.00, i.e. 50 Naira.
What number shows on my recepient mobile when I share a broadcast?
When you share a broadcast, the mobile number you registered on NaatCast would be the caller ID at the recepient end. The Called party would see your number and not a random or fake number.
How can I become a content provider?
To become a content provider, you need to be a registered user first. Login to your account, on the left-hand widget, move your mouse on My Subscription and the link to register as content provider would be revealed.
Click on the link and you would be led to the page where you need to submit some basic identification documents, an audio file sample of the content you want to be providing, and your content category.
After submission, you would receive an email from NaatCast once you've been approved. To know you've been approved, you would see a blue sticker on your profile picture when you login to your account, a new widget showing your subscriber counts would also be added to your left widgets. A new tab to share post with your subscribers would also be included in the drop down list of "Who can see this post?"
What is the benefit of being a content provider?
A profit share of ratio 60/40 on the amount generated by your contents.
Once you upload a content on NaatCast, your subscribers get notification, and when they make a request of the content, they get a call on their mobile phone and once they listen, they pay. A single content you uploaded once could be requested as many times as possible by your subscribers. They can also re-broadcast the content to anyone. The more your contents are requested, the more revenue for you as a content provider, and you can upload content as often as you like.
How do I change my profile picture?
To change your profile picture, go to profile setting, hover over the avatar and an edit button should appear. Click on it and follow through with the steps.
Who pays when I request for a content I subscribed to?
You pay when you request an audio from any content you subscribed to. You also pay when you re-broadcast a content to anyone.
Who pays when I send broadcast to my followers?
You pay when you broadcast any message to your followers. As a provider, your subscribers pay whenever they request for your content.
Can I follow someone without receiving audio broadcast from the person?
Yes. If you decide not to recieve audio broadcast from anyone you follow, navigate to that person's profile page, you'll see a check button marked with "No audio from this user" at the left hand widget. Check that button and you won't receive any audio broadcast from the user anymore. But this does not stop you from seeing all the update shared by the user on your timeline.
How do I recharge my account?
Navigate to profile settings, click on the drop down list of options at the center of the page, click on the payment history. You'll see the different payment options available.
How do I hide my mobile number?
You can hide or show a couple of information from other users. Navigate to the profile settings, you'll see edit button in front of all your data. Click on the edit button, and you'll be able to edit the field. Also you'll see check button in front of some of the data, checking and unchecking this would either hide or show that particular information to other users.
What is the purpose of the Alias in my profile page?
The alias is a way of providing opportunity for users to use their business name as their profile name. For example, if your business name is ABC Incorporated, you can use this as your alias and check the button to display the alias instead of your real name.
How can I drive traffic to my content from other social media as a content provider?
We've provided social media share tools for each broadcast made by a content provider. You as the content provider or your subscribers can utilize these tools to share your content to other social media. To access these tools, click on a content provider post in your timeline. You'll see sharing tools which you can use to drive traffic to your contents from other social media.
After changing my mobile number on profile page, I cannot recieve broadcast anymore
You would need to contact NaatCast if you want to change your mobile number. Making a change on the profile page is not enough, as we have to effect and approve the new number before you can use it. This is to prevent unauthorized random changing of mobile numbers on the platform.
